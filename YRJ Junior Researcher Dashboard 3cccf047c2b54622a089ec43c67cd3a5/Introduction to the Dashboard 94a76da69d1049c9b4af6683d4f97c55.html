Introduction to the Dashboard

General Information

This section contains general information about YRJ, its programs, plans, etc. I think the "YRJ Mission and Vision" section sufficiently explains itself. It is simply a section outlining the mission and vision.

To learn more about the program and its details, first go to the "Program Overview and Juniors Responsibilities" page. This will provide all the information you need to know about the program and its details, and what we except from you as a junior researcher.

Next, open the "Timeline" page which essentially has the program timeline in a table format for easy reading and understanding. It is divided into weeks, with each week divided into sessions and assignments so you can easily follow it.

Important Contacts

In the "Important Contacts" section, you will find two subsections: one for the Management Board and the other for the HR Committee. Each subsection contains the contact information of respective members along with their positions, making it easier for you to reach out to the appropriate person.

Contacting the Management Board:

Please use this contact information if you encounter an issue that specifically requires the attention and resolution of the Management Board. We encourage you to contact them only for significant matters that cannot be resolved by other means.

Contacting the HR Committee:

If you come across an issue related any HR-related matter, please use the contact information provided for the HR Committee. They will be better equipped to handle such situations and provide necessary guidance.

Task Submission Portal

This section provides quick access to the Google form you can use to submit each assigned task.
When you need to submit a task, simply:

  1. Open this portal
  1. Navigate to the relevant task
  1. Click the link to open the form
  1. Fill out the form following the instructions
  1. Submit your completed task

The form for each task will guide you through the submission process. Just open the link, fill it out, and submit.

Feedback Form

If you would like to provide feedback about your mentor, it would be very helpful for them and for us to improve the experience for future juniors. Providing feedback is optional, and rest assured that no details identifying you will be shared with anyone else. The only people who will know your identity are the management board.

Report Form

Our anonymous misconduct reporting system provides a safe space for junior researchers to report any improper conduct by their research mentor or supervisor. Your identity will be kept strictly confidential, accessible only to the management board responsible for handling these reports. Through this platform, you can voice concerns about violations of academic or moral integrity, unprofessional behavior, bullying, or harassment. Prompt action will be taken to address the issues and ensure a fair and supportive research environment for all. Your report helps us maintain the highest standards of integrity and professionalism within our research community.
